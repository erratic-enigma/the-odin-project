Indian Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Creamy, succulent chicken curry. Works well with fresh naan bread and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cut skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 chopped small onion
  • 1 cup of plain yoghurt
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • ¾ cup of coconut milk
  • ½ juiced lemon
  • ½ teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon of grated fresh ginger root
  • ½ teaspoon of white sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over a medium heat hob. Sauté the onion until it is lightly browned.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yoghurt and coconut milk.
  4. Take to a boil, reduce the heat and let it simmer for around 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Remove the bay leaf, then stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper.
  6. Allow dish to simmer for around 5 minutes before serving.
