Baked Potato Recipe

Description

A crisp, golden skin, contrast with a light and fluffy inside. For added kick, serve with a dollop of sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sized baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 1 pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup of shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat an oven to 150℃ / 300℉.
  2. Scrub the potato and then pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork.
  3. Rub the potato skin with olive oil, followed by salt.
  4. Place the potato into the oven and bake for around 90 minutes.
  5. Remove the potato from the oven when it is slightly soft and golden brown.
  6. Partially slice the potato down the center.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese atop the potato.
  8. Serve the potato alongside the butter and black pepper.
