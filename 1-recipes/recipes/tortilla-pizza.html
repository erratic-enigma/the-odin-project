Tortilla Pizza Recipe

Description

Classic cheese and tomato tortilla pizza. Good for groups or an appetiser. Experiment with different toppings for variety.

Ingredients

  • 20㎝ / 8″ soft flour tortillas
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 Pinch of garlic powder seasionings
  • Ground and salt black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 cooked sliced chicken breast
  • ½ chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 finely chopped green onions
  • ⅓ cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch of dried oregano
