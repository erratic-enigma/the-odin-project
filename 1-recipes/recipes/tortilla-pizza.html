Tortilla Pizza Recipe

Description

Classic cheese and tomato tortilla pizza. Good for groups or an appetiser. Experiment with different toppings for variety.

Ingredients

  • 20㎝ / 8″ soft flour tortillas
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1 Pinch of garlic powder seasionings
  • Ground and salt black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 cooked sliced chicken breast
  • ½ chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 finely chopped green onions
  • ⅓ cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch of dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat an oven to 200℃ / 400℉.
  2. Place the tortilla on a baking sheet.
  3. Brush the tortilla with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle garlic powder, salt and pepper atop the tortilla.
  5. Place the baking sheet into the oven and wait until the tortilla is golden, around 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Remove the baking sheet from the oven.
  7. Spread tomato sauce on top the tortilla, arrange the chicken breast, green bell pepper and green onions, then finally sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Place the baking sheet into the oven and wait until the cheese has melted, around 5 minutes.
  9. Remove the baking sheet from the oven and sprinkle oregano over the cheese.
  10. Slice the pizza using a knife or pizza cutter.
